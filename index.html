Water My Plants

Input Plants
Water My Plants allows you to input an unlimited amount of house plants to be available for reminders. The app will allow you to input the species of the plant, the amount of sunlight it needs / its location in your house, its age, and its size. You have the ability to see a list of your current plants as well as previous plants you no longer have.
Personalized Reminders
Once you have input your plants into Water My Plants, it will generate personalized reminders for when your plant needs to be watered and how much it needs to be watered. You can recieve your personalized reminders on the lock screen or notification bar of your phone or you can link it to your calendar.
Watering Schedule
Water My Plants creates a customized watering schedule for each plant you input into the app. We know how hard it can be to remember what plants need to be watered and when. That's why we created this feature that makes keeping your precious house plants alive a breeze.
Tips and Tricks
From beginner plant parents to advanced plant parents, Water My Plants gives you tips and tricks on how to keep your house plants happy and healthy. From learning how to pick the right planter for your plants to learning which plants are compatible with each other, this nifty feature has all the information you need to raise happy, healthy house plants.
